"SURAH IKHLAS" TRANSLITERATION AND TRANSLATION

About Surah Ikhlas: Surah Ikhlas (Arabic text: الْإِخْلَاص‎) is the 112th Surah of the Qur’an. It is titled in English “Sincerity” and composed of 4 verses...

In the name of Allah, the Entirely Merciful, the Especially Merciful.

Ayat 1st

Say, “He is Allah, [who is] One,

Ayat 2nd

Allah, the Eternal Refuge.

Ayat 3rd

He neither begets nor is born,

Ayat 4th

Nor is there to Him any equivalent.”
